What we do

Three pillars, one mission

Everything at EEHEO grows from an integrated approach — care, education, and community that reinforce one another.

01

Healthcare & Care

HIV/AIDS drug assistance, chronic care management, and holistic services that follow people through every stage of treatment.

02

Education & Empowerment

Child sponsorship, scholastic support, and youth-led programs that turn vulnerability into opportunity.

03

Community Strengthening

Linkage to care, advocacy, and grassroots mobilization that rebuild confidence and belonging.

Our mission
To better the livelihoods of vulnerable and disadvantaged groups through information sharing and coordinated service delivery — building a future in which they live with renewed hope and dignity.
